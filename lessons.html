Music Lessons

Private Lessons

Private lessons are a great way to learn music. We currently only offer lessons in Piano, but we are actively working towards expanding at a future date.

Piano Curriculum

We believe that to truly understand and appreciate music, one must have a well-rounded music education. Because of this, our piano curriculum is made up of several different components: Pieces (of course!), Scales and Arpeggios, Music Theory, Sight-Reading, and Aural Training.

Each of these is taught in a progressive and engaging manner. As students progress throught their respective 'levels', their understanding of music will sky-rocket, thanks to their knowledge of keys, patterns, tonalities, rhythms, styles, and more!
