About Us

The Acacia Academy of Music is a family-owned and operated music school located in Rogersville, Alabama. We offer high-quality piano lessons to children. Our goal is to develop the musical capabilities of each and every student, enabling them to become the best musicians that they can be. Each lesson is tailored to fit the individual needs and interests of the student. We seek to establish a strong technical and musical foundation to enable our students to progress rapidly and confidently. Above all, we seek to nurture our students' love and appreciation for music!

Keeping with our dedication to nurture musicianship, we offer a free trial lesson to all of our prospective students. We also offer our students the opportunity to take exams from various prestigious examination boards, allowing them to receive international recognition for their achievements.

Currently, we only offer lessons in Piano, but we are actively working towards expanding our offerings.

Contact us to find out more about pricing or to schedule a consultation!


Meet Our Instructor

Morgan Nogradi is an advanced pianist and instructor. She began her piano studies at the age of 8. Through her work with various teachers, she have been exposed to many different piano methods and has experience performing a variety of genres. Having been presented with a Distinction at Grade 8 in Piano Performance from the Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM), she is currently pursuing the Associate of the Royal Schools of Music (ARSM) diploma in Piano Performance from the same, along with a certification in Grade 7 Music Theory.

She is dedicated to seeing her students excel and offers them the opportunity to take exams from various prestigious examination boards. This allows them to showcase their work and achievements through certifications and awards. Some exams even count for high school and college credit!
