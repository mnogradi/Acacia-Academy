Acacia Academy of Music

About Us

Acacia Academy is a family-owned and operated music school located in beautiful Rogersville, Alabama. We offer private lessons to students of all ages. Our goal is to develop the musical capabilities of each and every student, enabling them to become the best musicians that they can be. Each lesson is tailored to fit the individual needs and interests of the student. We seek to establish a strong technical and musical foundation to enable our students to progress rapidly and confidently. Above all, we seek to nurture our students' love and appreciation for music!

Currently, we only offer lessons in piano, but we are actively working towards expanding our offerings.

Contact us to find out more about pricing or to schedule a consultation!


Meet Our Instructor

Morgan Nogradi is pianist and instructor. To date, she has been studying piano for 10 years. Through her work with various teachers, she have been exposed to many different piano methods and has experience performing a variety of genres. Having been presented with a Distinction at Grade 8 in Piano Performance from the Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM), she is currently pursuing the Associate of the Royal Schools of Music (ARSM) diploma in Piano Performance from the same, along with a certification in Grade 7 Music Theory.

Thanks to her dedication to seeing her students exel, she offers them the opportunity to take exams from various prestigious examination boards. This allows them to showcase their work and achievements through certifications and awards. Some exams even count for high school and college credit!
