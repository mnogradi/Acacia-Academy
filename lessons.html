Lesson Rates & Structure

Lessons are held weekly in Rogersville, AL or online. Every student receives individual instruction on a quality grand instrument, focused on developing the musicality and technique needed for steady development.

What to Expect

Introductory Lesson

All new students begin with a complimentary introductory lesson. This meeting provides an opportunity to discuss goals, evaluate current experience, and determine the best approach for continued musical study.

Flexible Lesson Format

Lessons may be taken in person or online. This allows for a hybrid approach—if scheduling becomes difficult, we can easily transition to an online session to ensure the student keeps progressing.
