What We Do:

We believe that a well-rounded music education is important to being a well-rounded musician. We currently only offer lessons in Piano. However, we are actively working towards expanding our offerings. Here are some of the core elements of our piano curriculem:

Intrumental Technique

Building a solid technical foundation is extremely important. Without good technique, it will be impossible for you to progress in a rapid and comfortable way. Through our in-depth focus on technique, we can teach you how to play with confidence and ease.

Scales and Arpeggios

Scales and Arpeggios are incredibly effective ways to develop your technique and muscle memory in your playing. They enable you to quickly grasp patterns and play fast, difficult passages with greater ease. They are a central part of all well-rounded instrumental studies.

Music Theory

Knowing the "musical grammer" of written music is incredibly helpful in understanding how to play any given piece. We help our students gain a solid foundation in the theoretical side of music to broaden their understandings of the pieces that they play.

Aural Training

Since music's 'domicile' is in our hearing (according to Heinrich Neuhaus), training our ears is absolutely crucial to musical success. Through our emphasis on the 'art of listening', we aim to increase our students' musical awareness and expressivity.

Proper Practice

Practicing effectively is the key to rapid progress. Throughout our students' studies, we aim to teach them not only WHAT to practice, but HOW to practice as well.

Musical Interpretation

Music is a language. The most important thing, therefore, is to learn how to express yourself through music. Through our in-depth approach to music education, we teach our students how to properly interpret and effectively communicate the music that they play.
