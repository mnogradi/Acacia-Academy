Music Lessons

Private Lessons

Private lessons are a fantastic way to learn an instrument and to deepen your appreciation for music. Acacia Academy offers private music lessons to children of various ages. At the Academy, we strive to tailor our approach to the individual needs and interests of our students, allowing them to become the best musicians that they can be.

Currently, we only offer lessons in Piano, but we hope to include other subjects in the future.

We offer a free trial lesson to all of our prospective students - contact us to find out more or to schedule a consultation!

Piano Curriculum

We believe that to truly understand and appreciate music, one must have a well-rounded music education. Because of this, our piano curriculum is made up of several different components: Pieces (of course!), Scales and Arpeggios, Music Theory, Sight-Reading, and Aural Training. These studies provide the solid foundation needed for our students to excel.

By approaching these studies in a progressive and engaging manner, we aim to develop the musicianship of the student while maintaining the joy of musical creativity.
